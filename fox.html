An elephant is the largest land animal, known for its big ears, long trunk, and tusks made of ivory. They use their trunks for breathing, smelling, grabbing objects, and drinking water. Elephants are highly intelligent and social animals, living in herds led by a matriarch. They are mostly found in Africa and Asia, roaming forests, grasslands, and savannas.
